Recent Projects

Foresight

Led the training and evaluation of Foresight, the world's first national AI healthcare model, a generative auto-regressive transformer model trained from scratch on 50+ million de-identified coded NHS England patient records. A general-purpose medical event prediction model, Foresight supports zero-shot predictions across a broad spectrum of medical events, including COVID-19, enabling scalable, data-driven clinical insights.

BioDEX: Adverse Drug Event Extraction

BioDEX is a large-scale dataset designed to enhance automated extraction of Adverse Drug Events (ADE) from biomedical literature using NLP, supporting more efficient pharmacovigilance. Benchmarking against human performance reveals both the challenges and promise of AI-assisted drug safety monitoring. A collaboration between Parexel AI Labs and Stanford University.

3D Infant Pose Estimation

This work introduces a transfer learning framework for estimating 3D infant poses from video using shape template transformations, addressing challenges in pediatric motion analysis. The method achieved state-of-the-art performance on benchmark datasets and offers potential for non-invasive infant monitoring.
