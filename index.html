Automated Discharge Summaries (AAAI 2024)

We present a system that generates hospital discharge summaries automatically by combining clinical guidelines with large language models, achieving improved coherence and accuracy over baseline methods. This approach reduces clinician workload and ensures consistent documentation across patient records.

BioDEX: Adverse Drug Event Extraction (EMNLP 2023)

BioDEX is a large-scale system for extracting adverse drug events from biomedical literature to support pharmacovigilance. It leverages transformer-based architectures trained on curated datasets and has been validated against real-world clinical trial data.

3D Infant Pose Estimation (NeurIPS Workshop 2020)

This work introduces a transfer learning framework for estimating 3D infant poses from video using shape template transformations, addressing challenges in pediatric motion analysis. The method achieved state-of-the-art performance on benchmark datasets and offers potential for non-invasive infant monitoring.
